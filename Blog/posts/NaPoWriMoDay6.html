NaPoWriMo Day 6


If an Uber driver wants to steal my car and drive to South Dakota
they're gonna be surprised that I've got a tracker on my car
and that's the location of all my boyfriend's delinquent, childhood friends
And he's down for some shady shit with dudes with scars from
headbutting their way out of conversations that were too boring.

And right know I'm doing weight lifties just thinking about it
and I'm up on my protein, I've been taking vegan supplements for six months.
I keep a shovel in the trunk of my car that folds in half.
I've got pink pepper spray on my keychain and if I go out
walking after midnight in a bad neighborhood
I keep a hammer up my sleeve. Not a knife, a hammer,
because I got shit to do
and I don't have time to be murdered.

I called out the meanest girl at school and when she said, "meet me outside,"
I said, "you better be ready to die." She never showed up,
and I still go to the school yard at 4PM each night waiting to get a swing at her.

If an Uber driver wants to steal my car and drive to South Dakota
they better have chainmail under their clothes and bullet proof glass.
I've got a case of lucky stripes and I'm aching to get back on my Harley,
because I'm not afraid of cancer or motorcycle death and I could think of motorcycle names that are more obscure, but who's gonna make me?

I got my blackest eyeliner on, and a spike necklace I took off a Rottweiler I wrestled, just because I wanted to give it some pets
and you bet your ass I'm not going to South Dakota without her, Uber driver.

Posted April 9, 2019
