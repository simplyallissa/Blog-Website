Welcome to the New Website


I've been working on a few websites the past year and leaning a lot. I finally decided to get my own running. Web design is something I've really come to love. More and more I've been getting raised eyebrows. "But, what about writing?" There will be time, there will be time. And to that I say, chill. I've got a lot of stuff to do, and sometimes it's okay if you're not trying to race the clock. I don't want to waste my time going down the one way streets. And besides, expectations were never really my thing. So I guess this is my way of saying, yes, I am still writing, I have still been writing, I'm gonna keep writing, but maybe not in the way that you had hoped or expected. I hope you'll stay a while. Someone pour another drink.
